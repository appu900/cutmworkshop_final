what is

Biosimulation ?

Biosimulation is a computational modelling approach used in drug development that enables researchers to predict the safety and efficacy profile of drugs in a variety of potential scenarios by conducting virtual clinical trials. It revolutionizes drug development by accelerating the discovery and testing of new drugs, improving their efficacy and safety, and reducing the overall cost of bringing new drugs to market.

Drug development can be accelerated by biosimulation in a number of ways

Target Audiences

The course is aimed at students, researchers, and technicians who wish to include genomics and NGS data analysis in their work but may not have sufficient training in/awareness of the required workflows. Ph.D. scholars/postdoctoral fellows/JRFs/faculty involved in pure experimental biological research/clinical research are encouraged to apply. The course will also be beneficial to those pursuing M.Sc./M. Tech. in Biotechnology/Life Sciences/Microbiology/Biosciences/medical sciences who may be interested in transitioning to bioinformatics/biosimulation-focused opportunities in academia or industry.
